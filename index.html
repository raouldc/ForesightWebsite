Revolutionising the fight against animal poaching

Realtime Notifications

The real-time detection and instant notifications provided by the Foresight system, gives anti-poaching teams the opportunity for a rapid response. This dramatically increases the chances of intruders being apprehended before the killing takes place, saving the lives of these innocent animals.

Human Detection

Foresight only sends out an alert with the detection of a human presence, eliminating the chances of false positives caused by animal movement, to ensure the most efficient and effective use of response resources.

Cost effective

Current anti-poaching technology is extremely costly, with many limitation that hinder their effectiveness. Foresight is the most cost effective solution that provides an all-in-one detection and notification system for wildlife authorities and anti-poaching units.

What We Do

Within our lifetime, the only rhinos, tigers, and elephants we will see, will be the fossils hanging in a museum. Animal poaching is a multibillion dollar industry resulting in thousands of animals killed every year. Almost 30,000 elephants were illegally slaughtered last year alone, with conservationists foreseeing their extinction within the next 15 years.

Foresight technology aims to provide a system which can actively and effectively detect and prevent poaching in conservation areas. The Foresight system uses Kinect sensors to enable a 24/7 surveillance around entry points and other "poaching hotspots" of a conservation area.

This surveillance system works by differentiating humans from animals which will aid in the detection of an unauthorized human personnel attempting to break into the conservation area. Once a detection occurs, the system will send an instant mobile notification to rangers and response teams to alert them of the possible poaching scenario.
